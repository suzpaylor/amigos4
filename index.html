Max Wilets


I get nervous in large crowds and and prefer more smaller more intimate situations that are less chaotic. Despite this I still enjoy going to concerts. Just practice deep breathing! I also get anxious when I order packages because I can't wait for them to arrive!

My greatest developer moment was working on the East High Support project. It was very rewarding to spend so much time and produce something that made a positive impact to the community.

My favorite pop culture moment is when Tony Hawk did the 900 for the first time. It was a moment that changed skateboard forever resulting in larger coverage from the media and a skateboarding video game franchise. Also the Avengers Infinity War and End Game was an amazing culmination of 10+ years of comic book movies.
